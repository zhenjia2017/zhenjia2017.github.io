BRIEF BIOGRAPHY

Zhen Jia is now a Lecturer/Assistant Professor at the School of Computing and Artificial Intelligence, Southwest Jiaotong University (SWJTU). I was a research visitor in the Max-Planck Institute for Informatics. In recent years, I has been the main researcher of the projects supported by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, Sichuan Science and Technology Program, and international collaboration research projects. I has published more than 60 papers in international and domestic academic journals and conferences, and has obtained 2 invention patents and 5 computer software copyrights. I am the deputy president of the Natural Language Processing Committee and the member of the Big Data Committee of the Sichuan Computer Society. I won the third prize of the Tibet Autonomous Region Science and Technology Award in 2018.

RESEARCH INTERESTS   Go Top

  • My research interests include natural language processing, question answering, information extraction, and knowledge graph, etc.
  • Now I focus on developing complex question answering and information retrieval in vertical domain to achieve domain-oriented chatbot system.
  • Ph.D. topic: Research on Ontology Learning and Knowledge Acquisition from Chinese Online Encyclopedia
